Introductory Tour of Archive.org and its Collections

  • December 31, 2020 23:25
  • Updated
Follow

The Internet Archive is a nonprofit library founded to gather knowledge in digital formats and make it available to everyone in the world for free. We have spent years building up collections of books, web pages, TV, movies, radio, concerts, etc.  But 40+ million objects can be a daunting library to navigate.

This video provides:

  • An overview of the major collections on archive.org and how to use them
  • Tips for how to efficiently find what you’re looking for
  • Pointers for where to find more information on your own

Make sure you’re ready to take notes, there’s a lot of information to share!
